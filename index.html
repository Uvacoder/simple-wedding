How to get around Boston

Using public transportation

Day train/bus passes can be purchased at most subway station kiosks for the MBTA, Boston's subway system. You can also use Lyft, a popular ride-hailing app.

The AC Hotel is steps away from the Reservoir and Cleveland Circle stops. Both stops are on the Green Line (C and D lines).

You can get to Spontaneous Celebrations via the Orange Line, taking a Forest Hills train to the Stony Brook stop. The venue is a ~3 minute walk from there.

Parking

Parking at the venue is limited and will be reserved for event staff and guests with mobility considerations. There is public street parking near the Stony Brook stop.
